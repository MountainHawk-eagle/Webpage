UK-INDIA FUTURE FORUM (UKIFF)

UKIFF is the most effective platform for businesses looking to optimise and grow their engagement within the UK-India corridor. It facilitates high-level engagement between business leaders and policy makers.

Through its annual calendar of open and closed door programmes, events, reports, briefings, and delegations, UKIFF works to advance strategic partnerships and investment flows between the two countries, and wider markets. Set piece calendar events include UK-India Week, the UK-India Parliamentary Lunch and Westminster Diwali Reception.

Foundations

IGF has deep roots in the UK with earlier iterations of the platform stemming back to 2014. Since then, our events have hosted Prime Minister Modi and numerous Indian cabinet ministers, HM King Charles III, and five serving and former British Prime Ministers.
